Our Story ...

We are all Computer Science graduates from IIIT Delhi. The thought that people go to movies by just taking in the opinions of movie critiques frustrated us. We believe there is a movie critique in each one of us in the crowd and if a person takes into account this diverse opinion, he would be able to make a much more informed decision of watching a given movie in theaters. And here we come up with our app called TweetMyFlick that gives an aggregated sentiment of any Bollywood movie from Twitter community
